### WaffleStudio Toy Project
Dec 2022 - Feb 2023 \
*Web & App Development Project hosted by Seoul National University*

<details>
<p><b>Project Description</b>
WaffleStudio is one of the largest development clubs in Seoul National University. Annually, the club's leadership arranges a toy project session where participants engage in an extensive development process. This involves initial team formation, project ideation, followed by the execution and deployment of the service for public access. At the end of the session, teams are evaluated and prizes are awarded to those achieving top ranks.</p>

<p><b>Contributions</b>
Our team developed a sharable calendar web service using Next.js and Django. Through this platform, users can efficiently organize their schedules, create posts linked to specific events, and share these schedules to get comments from others. I played a role as a frontend developer. Our team secured the third position among all the competing participants.</p>
